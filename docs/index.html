PSE&G Programs

The Right Program for Every Facility

PSE&G offers five distinct incentive programs — each structured for a different facility size and profile. Lumens America navigates all of them.

P
Any Size Business
Prescriptive Program

Fixed rebates per unit for pre-approved LED fixtures and HVAC equipment. Fastest path to money back — no custom engineering required.

DI
Small-Medium Facilities
Direct Install Program

PSE&G installs equipment at no upfront cost for qualifying small businesses. Lumens America handles the entire application and coordination.

ES
Larger Projects
Engineered Solutions Tier 2

Custom-engineered projects with incentives tied to actual measured energy savings. Best for complex HVAC and large lighting retrofits.

On-Bill Repayment (OBR) Financing

PSE&G's OBR program lets qualifying customers finance upgrades at 0% interest, repaid over 60 months directly on their utility bill. In most cases, monthly energy savings exceed the repayment amount from day one.
